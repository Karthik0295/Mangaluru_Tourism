Water activities around Mangalore

Mangalore is a coastal city located in the southern state of Karnataka in India. It offers plenty of water activities for tourists and locals alike. Here are some popular water activities to do in Mangalore:

Explore

1. Fising

Fishing is a well-liked pastime in the area, and Mangalore is well-known for its fishing business. You may enjoy fishing at a number of locations in and around Mangalore. If you're going on a fishing trip, you'll need to bring your own equipment or rent it from a local vendor. Several different fish species, including as mackerel, barracuda, tuna, and snapper, may be found in Mangalore. The type of fish you'll catch will depend on the season and location.

2. Surfing

The ocean is vast and beautiful with an air of adventure. Surfing is not about conquering its deep waters but creating a sense of oneness with this spectacular water body and all it holds. Indica Surf School helps in developing, building and growing surfing and aquatic sports. From beginners to professional surfers, there’s something for everyone. Uncover the magic of an aquatic playground through expert guidance and knowledge.

3. Kayaking

Witness the beautiful backwaters of the Shambahvi River in Mulki near Mangalore while you kayak around Nadikudru island. The river rides on the kayaks are complemented by the pristine scenery with beautiful mangrove forests, colorful wildlife, and coconut plantations on either side of the river.

4. Cliff Jumping

Malpe is a beautiful beach located near Udupi in Karnataka, India, and cliff jumping is a popular activity among tourists and locals in this area. Standing at the top of the cliff and staring into the Arabian sea alone is enough to send shivers down the spine.

5. Parasailing

Parasailing in Malpe is a thrilling sport that is very popular and much sought after. With a wide array of water and land sports, this land of beaches is undoubtedly one of the best places to try water sports in India. Parasailing is a popular water sport and a thrilling activity which is enjoyed by tourists from all over the world.

6. Scuba Diving, Netrani Island

Also known as the 'Pigeon Island', Netrani Island in Murudeshwar is located off the coast of Karnataka. If you are someone who loves to explore nature in every way possible, you should also try Scuba Diving! Exploring the depths of the sea and swimming with underwater life is a unique experience in itself. An alien world for many, scuba diving brings you closer to a new world of coral reefs, beautiful and different species fish, flora and fauna of the marine world.

7. Jet Ski, Panambur

Panambur beach offers extensive variety of water sports for the tourists who want to sense the thrill and jet ski ride is one of the water sports that can perfectly quench your thirst for adventure and thrill. If you are fond of speed then this water sport is made for you. Jet ski ride includes a jet powered water scooter which will take you to the spectacular nearby places on the beach, you will feel the chill through your veins while riding

Floating Bridge

8. Floating Bridge, Malpe

Floating bridge in Malpe is not fixed to ground using pillars. Thus as waves come in, bridge flexes up and down, slightly throwing visitors off balance and giving a sense of adventure. Stronger the waves, more the fun. Now tourists have one more adventure activity to add in their list because this floating bridge’s fun is a must not miss. Due to the rise in tourist footfall, it is already emerging as one of the best tourist attractions to visit in Malpe.
